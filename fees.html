Frequently Asked Questions

Is Sri Gnanajyothi School accepting LKG admissions for 2026-27?

Yes, Sri Gnanajyothi School is accepting admissions for LKG, UKG and all classes from I to X for the 2026-27 academic year. Contact us at +91 90358 44103 to enquire about available seats.

Which board does Sri Gnanajyothi School follow?

Sri Gnanajyothi School follows the Karnataka State Board (KSEEB – Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board) curriculum. We are a Government of Karnataka recognised English medium school in Yelahanka, Bangalore.

What is the school fee for LKG and UKG at Gnanajyothi School?

Fee details for LKG, UKG and all classes are available on request. Call us at +91 90358 44103 or email sgjeschool@gmail.com for the current 2026-27 fee structure.

Where is Sri Gnanajyothi School located in Yelahanka?

Sri Gnanajyothi School is located at Gnanada Gudi, Gnanarathna Dr. D. A. Gowda Sir School Road, Yelahanka Agrahara Badavane, Bengaluru – 560064. It is easily accessible from Yelahanka, Agrahara, and surrounding areas of North Bangalore.

Does the school provide transport / van facilities?

Please contact the school at +91 90358 44103 to enquire about transport and van facility availability for your area in Yelahanka and North Bangalore.

Are there any scholarships or sibling discounts?

Yes, Sri Gnanajyothi School offers sibling discounts and merit-based scholarships. Contact us for eligibility details.
