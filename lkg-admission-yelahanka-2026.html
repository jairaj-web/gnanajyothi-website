LKG Age Criteria for 2026-27 in Karnataka

The Karnataka government has set a minimum age requirement for LKG admission. For the 2026-27 academic year:

LKG (Lower KG)

Min. 3 years 6 months as of June 1, 2026

UKG (Upper KG)

Min. 4 years 6 months as of June 1, 2026

Class 1

Min. 5 years 6 months as of June 1, 2026

Most schools in Yelahanka strictly follow these age guidelines as per Karnataka DDPI norms. Make sure your child meets the age requirement before applying.

Documents Required for LKG Admission in Yelahanka

Keep these documents ready before visiting the school for admission:

  • Child's original birth certificate (municipal corporation / hospital)
  • Aadhaar card of the child (if available)
  • Aadhaar card of both parents / guardians
  • Recent passport size photographs of the child (4–6 copies)
  • Address proof (electricity bill, rental agreement, or Aadhaar)
  • Caste certificate (if applying for SC/ST fee concession)
  • Parent's income certificate (if required for fee waiver)

LKG Admission Process — Step by Step

  1. Visit the school — Meet the admission office and collect the application form. At Sri Gnanajyothi School, you can also call +91 90358 44103 to schedule a visit.
  2. Fill the application form — Complete the form with your child's and parent's details.
  3. Submit documents — Attach all required documents (listed above) along with the application.
  4. Pay the registration fee — A nominal registration fee is charged at most schools.
  5. Interaction/observation — Some schools conduct a brief informal interaction with the child (not an exam).
  6. Admission confirmation — Once seat is confirmed, pay the fees and collect the admission letter.

What to Look for in a Good LKG School in Yelahanka

Choosing the right pre-primary school sets the foundation for your child's learning. Here is what to check:

  • Qualified and experienced early childhood educators
  • Safe, child-friendly and hygienic campus
  • Activity-based learning approach (play-way method)
  • Small class sizes for individual attention
  • CCTV surveillance and secure entry/exit
  • Government recognised and DDPI affiliated
  • Clear and transparent fee structure
  • Good track record in the community
