Beyond Academics

Extracurricular Activities

We believe in nurturing every talent. Our students participate in a wide range of activities that build confidence, creativity, and leadership.

Sports

Cricket, Football, Basketball, Kabaddi, Kho-Kho and Athletics on our spacious sports ground.

Music & Dance

Classical and folk music, traditional dance forms, and cultural performances at school events.

Art & Craft

Drawing, painting, clay modelling, and craft activities to develop creativity and fine motor skills.

Chess & Mind Games

Chess club and puzzle activities that sharpen critical thinking and concentration from an early age.

Yoga & Meditation

Daily yoga and mindfulness sessions for physical fitness, mental well-being, and focus.

Computer Education

Hands-on computer lab sessions covering digital literacy, basic programming, and MS Office skills.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about admissions, programs, and our school.

Where is Sri Gnanajyothi School located?

Sri Gnanajyothi School is located at Gnanada Gudi, Gnanarathna Dr. D. A. Gowda Sir School Road, Yelahanka Agrahara Badavane, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560064. The school is easily accessible by road and public transport.

Is Gnanajyothi School accepting admissions for 2026-27?

Yes, Sri Gnanajyothi School is accepting admissions for 2026-27 from LKG to Class 10. Contact us at +91 90358 44103 or email sgjeschool@gmail.com to enquire or schedule a visit.

When was Sri Gnanajyothi School established?

Sri Gnanajyothi School was established in 1989 and has over 37 years of excellence in providing quality education to students in Yelahanka, Bangalore.

What facilities does Gnanajyothi School offer?

Sri Gnanajyothi School offers smart classrooms, a fully equipped science laboratory, computer lab, well-stocked library, sports ground, and dedicated rooms for art and music activities.

What are the school timings at Gnanajyothi School?

Sri Gnanajyothi School office is open Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. The office is available for admissions enquiries and campus visits during these hours.
