Academic Programs

Programs for Every Stage

Our structured academic programs are tailored to the needs of students at every level, ensuring a smooth, supported journey from primary to secondary education.

Pre-Primary

LKG & UKG (Ages 3–5)

Our Pre-Primary programme gives your child the warmest, most nurturing start to school life. Through play-based learning, stories, songs, and hands-on activities, we build confidence, curiosity, and foundational skills.

  • Play-based and activity-centred learning
  • Early literacy — letters, phonics, and reading readiness
  • Number sense and basic Maths concepts
  • Art, craft, music, and movement activities
  • Social skills, sharing, and classroom etiquette

Primary School

Grades I – V

Our Primary program lays the essential foundation for lifelong learning. Through engaging, play-based activities and structured academics, we build literacy, numeracy, and a love of learning in young minds aged 6 to 11.

  • Phonics-based English language instruction
  • Hands-on Maths and Environmental Science
  • Hindi, Kannada, and value education
  • Art, Music, and Physical Education
  • Regular parent-teacher interaction

Middle School

Grades VI – VIII

The Middle School years are pivotal. Our curriculum deepens subject knowledge while encouraging critical thinking, teamwork, and independent study skills — setting students up for success in secondary school and beyond.

  • Comprehensive Science and Social Studies
  • Advanced Mathematics and Language Arts
  • Introduction to computer science and coding
  • Project-based learning and group work
  • Leadership and personality development

Secondary School

Grades IX – X

Our Secondary program prepares students for the Karnataka SSLC Board examinations with focused academics, rigorous practice, and personalised attention. We aim for both academic excellence and holistic growth.

  • KSEEB-aligned curriculum with expert faculty
  • Regular mock tests and board exam preparation
  • Science, Commerce, and Humanities streams
  • Career guidance and counselling sessions
  • Competitive exam awareness programs

Our Approach

How We Teach

We go beyond textbooks. Our teaching philosophy is rooted in engagement, inclusion, and continuous growth — because every child learns differently.

Activity-Based Learning

We use experiments, projects, games, and real-world challenges to make abstract concepts tangible and memorable, sparking genuine curiosity in every child.

Individual Attention

Small class sizes and dedicated teachers ensure every student receives personalised guidance, support, and encouragement suited to their unique learning pace.

Regular Assessment

Continuous and comprehensive evaluation through tests, assignments, and projects keeps students and parents informed about progress and areas of improvement.

Extracurricular Activities

Sports, arts, music, debates, and cultural events complement academics, developing confidence, creativity, and social skills in every student.
