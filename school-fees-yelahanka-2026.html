School Fees in Yelahanka 2026-27
What Parents Need to Know

March 2026 4 min read Yelahanka, Bangalore

One of the first questions parents ask when looking for a school in Yelahanka is: "How much are the school fees?" This guide breaks down everything you need to know about school fees in Yelahanka for 2026-27 — what is typically included, how fees differ by board, and what to watch out for.

Always ask for a complete written fee breakup before enrolling. A transparent school will happily provide this.

Types of Fees Charged by Schools in Yelahanka

School fees are rarely just one single amount. Here is a breakdown of the different charges you may encounter:

  • Tuition Fee — Core academic instruction fee. Paid monthly, quarterly or annually.
  • Admission / Registration Fee — One-time fee paid when joining. Non-refundable in most schools.
  • Development Fee — For infrastructure and facility maintenance.
  • Examination Fee — For internal and board exams (Class 10 SSLC).
  • Uniform Fee — School uniform, belt, tie, ID card.
  • Books and Stationery — Textbooks, notebooks, and school supplies.
  • Sports / Activity Fee — For sports, art, music, and co-curricular activities.
  • Transport Fee — If you opt for school bus facility (optional).

Karnataka State Board vs CBSE Fees in Yelahanka

Board affiliation significantly affects the fee level. Here is a general comparison for Yelahanka schools in 2026-27:

Questions to Ask Before Paying School Fees

  1. Is the fee structure available in writing?
