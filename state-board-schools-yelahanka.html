What is the Karnataka State Board?

The Karnataka State Board, officially known as KSEEB (Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board), is the government-recognised education board for schools in Karnataka. It governs the curriculum, textbooks and examinations for students from primary school through to Class 10 (SSLC).

State board schools in Yelahanka follow the KSEEB curriculum, which is available in both English and Kannada medium. English medium state board schools are the most popular choice for families in Yelahanka who want affordable, quality education with local relevance.

Why Choose a State Board School in Yelahanka?

1. Most Affordable Board in Bangalore

Karnataka State Board school fees are significantly lower than CBSE or ICSE schools. For families in Yelahanka looking for quality education without heavy fees, state board schools offer the best value.

2. NEP 2020 Aligned Curriculum

KSEEB has aligned its curriculum with the National Education Policy 2020, emphasising holistic development, foundational literacy, critical thinking and multilingual education from primary school onwards.

3. Best for Karnataka CET and State Exams

If your child plans to pursue engineering, medicine or other professional courses in Karnataka, the state board curriculum directly prepares students for the Karnataka Common Entrance Test (CET) — giving them a natural advantage over students from other boards.

4. Strong Local Network

State board schools in Yelahanka are well connected with the local community, offering a familiar and supportive environment for students from the area. The schools understand local needs and provide culturally relevant education.

5. Recognised Across Karnataka

KSEEB credentials are recognised and respected by all Karnataka colleges and universities. Students from state board schools go on to pursue engineering, medicine, law, commerce and arts at top Karnataka institutions.
