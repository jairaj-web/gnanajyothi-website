How to Choose the Right School in Yelahanka

Here is a simple step-by-step checklist for parents planning school admissions in Yelahanka for 2026-27:

  1. Check board recognition — Make sure the school is DDPI recognised and affiliated to a valid board (KSEEB, CBSE, ICSE).
  2. Visit the campus — A physical visit gives you the best picture of infrastructure, cleanliness, and safety.
  3. Meet the teachers — Speak to class teachers to understand their approach to education and discipline.
  4. Review the fee structure — Ask for a complete breakdown of all fees including transport, uniform and activity fees.
  5. Check student reviews — Talk to parents of current students in the school to get honest feedback.
  6. Apply early — Good schools in Yelahanka fill seats quickly. Apply before March end for 2026-27.

Karnataka State Board vs CBSE — Which is Better for Yelahanka?

Parents often ask whether to choose a Karnataka State Board (KSEEB) school or a CBSE school in Yelahanka. Here is a simple comparison:

Factor Karnataka State Board CBSE
Fees Generally more affordable Typically higher
Local recognition Excellent in Karnataka National level
Language Kannada + English English primary
NEP 2020 Fully aligned Aligned
Best for Local careers, CET, medicine, engineering in Karnataka Families who move frequently or prefer national curriculum

For most families settled in Yelahanka, Karnataka State Board is the practical and affordable choice with excellent outcomes for local competitive exams like CET.
