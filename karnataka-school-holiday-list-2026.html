Karnataka School Holiday List 2026-27
Complete Academic Calendar

March 2026 4 min read Karnataka

Planning your child's schedule and family vacations requires knowing the school holiday calendar in advance. Here is the complete Karnataka school holiday list for 2026-27 including public holidays, term vacations, and key dates for the academic year.

Note: Official holiday dates are declared by the Karnataka DDPI and state government. Dates below are based on the standard Karnataka school calendar pattern and may vary slightly by school.

Key Exam Dates — Karnataka State Board 2026-27

  • SSLC (Class 10) Board Exams — March 2027 (tentative)
  • Annual Exams (Class 1–9) — March–April 2027
  • Half-yearly Exams — September–October 2026
  • Unit Tests / Quarterly Exams — August 2026
  • KCET (for PUC students) — April–May 2027

Tips for Parents During School Holidays

  • Use summer vacation for enrollment and new school admissions — most schools complete admissions by May
  • Collect books and uniforms during Dasara vacation to prepare for Term 2
  • Plan family vacations around Dasara break (October) and Summer (April–June)
  • Check with your school for exact dates as they may vary by a few days

Frequently Asked Questions

When does summer vacation start in Karnataka schools 2026?

Summer vacation in Karnataka schools typically starts around April 22-25 and ends in mid-June when the new academic year begins.

When is Dasara holiday for Karnataka schools in 2026?

Dasara holiday for Karnataka schools in 2026 is expected around October 1-15. The exact dates are declared by the Karnataka government each year.

When does the new academic year start in Karnataka schools?

The new academic year for Karnataka state board schools typically starts in the second or third week of June, usually between June 12-20.
