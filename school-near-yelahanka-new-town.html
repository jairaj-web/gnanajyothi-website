About Yelahanka New Town

Yelahanka New Town (YNT) is a planned residential area in North Bangalore, developed by the Bangalore Development Authority (BDA). It is well connected to Yelahanka Old Town, Hebbal, Vidyaranyapura and the Outer Ring Road. With growing residential density and a family-friendly environment, demand for quality schools near Yelahanka New Town has increased significantly in recent years.

YNT residents benefit from proximity to both Yelahanka Agrahara (where many established schools are located) and newer school campuses along the Bellary Road corridor.

Admission Checklist for Schools Near Yelahanka New Town

When visiting any school near Yelahanka New Town for admission, carry the following documents:

  • Child's birth certificate (original + photocopy)
  • Aadhar card of child and both parents
  • 2–4 recent passport-size photographs of the child
  • Proof of residence (utility bill / rental agreement)
  • Previous school transfer certificate (for Class 2 and above)
  • Previous year's report card or mark sheet
  • Immunisation / vaccination certificate (for nursery/LKG)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable, for reservation benefits)
