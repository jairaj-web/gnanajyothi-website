Class 1 Admission Age in Karnataka 2026-27

March 2026 Updated March 2026 5 min read Bangalore, Karnataka

One of the most common questions parents in Bangalore and Yelahanka ask every year is: "What is the minimum age for Class 1 admission in Karnataka?" With new NEP 2020 guidelines and changing school policies, many parents are confused about the correct age cutoff. This guide gives you a clear, accurate answer for 2026-27 admissions.

Quick Answer: A child must be at least 6 years old as of June 1, 2026 to be eligible for Class 1 admission in Karnataka for the 2026-27 academic year.

Class 1 Age Requirement in Karnataka – NEP 2020 Rules

The National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 restructured the school education system in India. Under NEP 2020, the new structure is 5+3+3+4:

  • Foundational Stage (5 years): Pre-KG (2 years) + LKG, UKG, Class 1 (3 years)
  • Preparatory Stage (3 years): Class 2, 3, 4
  • Middle Stage (3 years): Class 5, 6, 7
  • Secondary Stage (4 years): Class 8, 9, 10, 11 + 12

Under this framework, a child ideally completes 2 years of pre-school (LKG + UKG) before entering Class 1. The minimum age for Class 1 in Karnataka is 6 years, as aligned with NEP 2020 and the Right to Education (RTE) Act.

What Happens If My Child Is Too Young for Class 1?

If your child will not turn 6 before June 1, 2026, they are generally not eligible for Class 1 in the 2026-27 academic year. In that case, the right step is to enroll them in UKG for 2026-27 and apply for Class 1 in the following year (2027-28) once they meet the age requirement.

This is actually beneficial — rushing a child into Class 1 before they are ready can affect their confidence and learning outcomes in the long run.

How to Check If Your Child Is Eligible for Class 1

  1. Note your child's date of birth
  2. Check if they will be 6 years old on or before June 1, 2026
  3. If yes — your child is eligible for Class 1 in 2026-27
  4. If no — enroll in UKG for 2026-27 and target Class 1 for 2027-28

Quick Examples

  • Child born on May 15, 2020 — Will be 6 on May 15, 2026 — Eligible for Class 1 in 2026-27
  • Child born on September 10, 2020 — Will be 6 on Sep 10, 2026 (after June 1) — Not eligible for Class 1; should join UKG
  • Child born on January 3, 2021 — Will be only 5 by June 1, 2026 — Not eligible; should join UKG

Documents Required for Class 1 Admission in Bangalore

  • Birth certificate (original)
  • UKG / Transfer Certificate from previous school
  • Previous year progress report / mark card
  • Aadhaar card of child and parent/guardian
  • 4 recent passport size photographs
  • Address proof (Aadhaar / utility bill)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable for reservations)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age for Class 1 admission in Karnataka 2026?

As per Karnataka government and NEP 2020 guidelines, a child must be at least 6 years old as of June 1, 2026 to be eligible for Class 1 admission in the 2026-27 academic year.

What is the age cutoff date for Class 1 in Karnataka?

The age cutoff date for Class 1 in Karnataka is June 1. For 2026-27 admissions, the child must complete 6 years of age on or before June 1, 2026.

Can a 5-year-old join Class 1 in Bangalore?

Generally, no. The minimum age for Class 1 in Karnataka is 6 years as of June 1. A child who has not yet turned 6 by June 1 would typically need to complete UKG first before joining Class 1.

What is the age for LKG admission in Karnataka 2026?

For LKG (Lower Kindergarten) admission in Karnataka 2026-27, the child should be at least 3.5 years old as of June 1, 2026. For UKG, the child should be at least 4.5 years old.
